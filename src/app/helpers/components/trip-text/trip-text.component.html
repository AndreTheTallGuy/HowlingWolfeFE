Sunset & S’mores Kayak Tour: $59/Person

Dates: TBD

Time: 6pm-7pm

Trip Duration: 1 Hour

Skill level: ALL

Cost: $59/person

Feed your adventurous spirit, as well as your hunger, on our deliciously sweet Sunset & S’mores guided kayak tour. This quick, family-friendly tour is perfect for all skill levels. The journey ends at the Athletic Club, where we provide the perfect sunset view on the Fox River, S’mores, and the opportunity to make new friends around the campfire.

The tour begins at the Aurora Athletic Club, where participants will park their cars and be shuttled up to North Aurora Island Park. Upon arrival at the park, participants will be given paddles and lifejackets, sized for kayaks, and receive dryland instruction for the following skills:

  • How to go straight
  • How to steer
  • How to stop
  • How to stay upright and comfortable

Once the instruction is over, the guide will assist you in getting your boat into the water, and you’ll be ready to start your trip! To be in a tandem kayak with an adult, children must be at least 30 pounds and follow directions well. To be in a single kayak, participants must be 15 years of age and at least 100lbs.

Howling Wolfe Canoe and Kayak Provides:

  • Kayak
  • Personal floatation device (PFD)
  • Shuttle
  • Paddle
  • Guide
  • S’mores
  • Sunset views

Trip Duration: 1 Hour

Skill level: ALL

Brother Chimp Brewery:
