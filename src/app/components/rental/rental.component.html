Paddlesport Rentals

Looking for a fun new activity for this summer? Curious about canoeing and kayaking? Experience the Fox River from a new vantage point with Howling Wolfe Canoe & Kayak this summer!

Howling Wolfe Canoe & Kayak offers both kayak and canoe rentals, lessons and guided trips on the Fox River in Aurora Illlinois!

The Fox River is home to many different species of wildlife and an abundance of flora and fauna! Howling Wolfe Canoe & Kayak will get you prepared for the Fox River experience of your choosing!

On Site: On site rentals are perfect for those who might be new to paddle sports and are looking for something short, easy and relaxing!

On site rentals launch at the Aurora Athletic Club, paddlers are welcome to start their journey by paddling up stream and floating down towards downtown Aurora with a short paddle back to the Athletic Club.

North Aurora Paddle: The North Aurora paddle is a great option for people who have never paddled on a river before or for those who are looking for a short float with no portages.
> Launch at North Aurora Island Park
> Takeout at Aurora Athletic Club

Batavia Paddle: The Batavia paddle is a great option for those folks who are looking for a half day adventure with lots of sightseeing, wildlife and plenty of time to paddle!
> Launch at Glenwood Forest Preserve
> (1 portage around North Aurora Dam)
> Takeout at Aurora Athletic Club
